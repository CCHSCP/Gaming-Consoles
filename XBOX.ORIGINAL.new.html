History

Microsoft started planning the release of an Xbox as early as 1998. The creators Kevin Bachus, Seamus Blackley, Otto Berkes and Ted Hase told Bill Gates about their idea in 1998, and he said go for it. The Xbox Original was really supposed to be call the DirectX Box, it was supposed to be designed like a PC, it would run off windows 2000, making it easy for people to work with it, it would feature twice the power a PlayStation has. Though they thought the name "xbox" wouldn't work but they stuck with it anyway.
After 2 years of development it was finally released in November of 2001.
